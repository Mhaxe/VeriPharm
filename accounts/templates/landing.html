How VeriPharm Works

FDA Monitoring

FDA monitors registered drugs and detects fraud/misuse using AI tools and data analytics.

Manufacturers

Manufacturers register drugs and generate secure QR codes tied to each product batch.

Distributors & Pharmacists

Each transfer is verified and logged to ensure traceability and integrity throughout the supply chain.

Patients

Patients scan QR codes to verify drug authenticity and report any suspicious or harmful products.
